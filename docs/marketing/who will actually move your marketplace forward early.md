
# 🎯 First, reality check (important)

For Billoria to work, you need **3 sides activated simultaneously**:

1. 🏢 **Inventory (Supply)** → billboard owners / agencies
2. 💰 **Demand (Buyers)** → brands / marketing teams
3. 🛠️ **Execution (Vendors)** → printing, installation, LED, etc.

👉 If you bring only brands → nothing to book
👉 If you bring only owners → no demand
👉 If you bring vendors → no transactions

So we target **specific people in each layer**.

---

# 🧠 1. HIGH PRIORITY: Agency Media Buyers (Your #1 Target)

These are the people who will **actually use Billoria weekly**.

### 🎯 Who exactly?

* Media Planning Manager
* Media Buying Executive
* Account Director (Agency side)

### 🏢 Target companies:

* Asiatic 360
* Mindshare Bangladesh
* GroupM Bangladesh
* Magnito Digital

### 👤 Example people to look up:

* Media planners at Asiatic
* Client servicing leads at Magnito
* Media buyers at Mindshare

👉 Why they matter:

* They handle **multiple brands at once**
* They constantly need **available billboards**
* They suffer from **manual coordination pain**

👉 Your pitch to them:

> “You can check availability, price, and book faster than calling 10 vendors.”
